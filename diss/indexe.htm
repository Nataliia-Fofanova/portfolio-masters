Introduction

The variety of presentation options, and post information on the site, and and lack of scientific study and systematization of relevant experience makes the task of developing algorithms and methods of quality control software (sites) extremely urgent. Implementation of the developed algorithms will ensure not only high quality of the final software product (site), but also improving the effectiveness of its use.

To perform the task need to highlight features of construction sites, taking into account a number of factors. These include: constructive presentation of information (usability of the site), high-quality eye-catching design, creative ideas and solutions, advanced content management functionality (amendments, additions and so forth.). Of particular importance is such a characteristic as basic features site optimization for search engines, and others.

Questions of the organization of quality control developed software products ( particular sites) are presented in the works of Russian and foreign authors as S. Dobrynin, Bosov AV and etc. [7], [8], [9].

The analysis revealed a difference in approach to the assessment of different sites authors. The proposed work is qualitatively different from the technique of the analytes, it is based on existing standards (ISO / IEC 9126-93 "Assessment software products ") and the maximum number of estimated parameters.

Furthermore, according to GOST R ISO / IEC 9126-93 "Evaluation of software products" [10], the site shall be of such quality characteristics as functional possibilities, reliability, usability, efficiency, maintainability and portability, and features inherent sites in accordance with data characteristics.

Functionalities include a number of characteristics, such as: the suitability, accuracy, interoperability, consistency, protection. Suitability characterizes the site in terms of its possession of the minimum featured (content). Correctness means that the site should accurately reflect and the data entered. Interoperability shows how the system It interacts with the operating system (in this security products cross-platform). Consistency describes the site in terms of compliance laws and rules of its design (for example: the law on the content of the site). Protection It shows how the site should be protected from the content unauthorized access.

Next a number of characteristics presented reliability and includes performance stability, fault tolerance and recoverability. Stability It means that the site has the smallest number of failures in the software error. Resistance to an error saying that the site has to maintain a certain level of quality in cases, software bugs. For example, the user has no support for flash PC and need to transfer all the information in flash in a different format for the User. Recoverability shows that when an error is able to quickly site restore its functionality (the site has no specific interaction with client and therefore has immediate error recovery at user).

Indicators practicality characterize understandability, learnability, simplicity use. Clarity means that the site should be clear and well perceived by the user. Learning suggests that the site contains a number of Tips for proper use of the website by the user. Ease of use It characterizes the site in terms of ease of navigation, simplicity and speed in finding necessary information.

One of the most important sets of characteristics including performance indicators. This characteristics such as: changes in the nature of resources and the nature of the change in time. They say that the site should be well optimized for fast operation.

Next inherent characteristic of software products - maintainability - presented figures testability, stability, variability analyzed. Testability means that the site should be easily verifiable with the modifications (For example, uses a technology called "Apache" to check the site on the local machine). Stability indicates that the site is protected from the risk of unsuccessful modifications i.e. roll back changes (any modification on the site can be removed manually from the code of the page). The variability characterizes the site in terms of the possibility of modification (for minimal modification site can use any text editor, except for a few cgi-scripts). It indicates the presence of the analyte at the site diagnostic devices self-empowerment and modifications necessary software to achieve the best performance (for example: if the site requires java or flash later, the user may be prompted to modify the software).

Last number of characteristics presented indicators of mobility and includes: adaptability, ease of implementation, compliance, interchangeability. Adaptability It means that the site should work on any software. Ease of implementation indicates that the site should function under any set of user software. Compliance - characteristics, to express to the site laws of the country in which it is used. Interchangeability indicates that the site is capable of substitution such resource without much difficulty.

One of the key points to consider when building a website - it that the Internet is generally a source of information. Thus, the site as part of Internet should provide as much information to visitors and users. In addition, visitors have easy to navigate in its pages. more site gives answers to the issues facing the visitors, the higher the probability that they will use it in the future.

Estimation of parameters such as traffic and demand for content is suitable and is effective only after a certain period of time the work site, so these figures will not be counted.[11]

1. Theme urgency

Today, information technologies have begun to actively influence the daily activities of any organization and become an integral component of its information infrastructure. Modern website [15], 16, [17] - This extensive system software, information, media resources, logically interrelated. Therefore, among the site developers highlight the category of professional researchers the quality of these products. The results of this assessment should reflect the characteristics of the quality of web sites and used as developers for certification of the product, and customers the acquisition of sites. The quality of sites is an integral characteristic, including a wide range of product properties and determines the degree of user satisfaction.

3. The system analysis of the process of evaluation of quality web development

3.1 Development of morphological model


Morphological model is a combination of the following models:

  • model is a "black box";
  • model composition of the system;
  • model of the structure of the system.

The model "black box" includes:

  • model border;
  • a model of the environment of the system;
  • Model inputs System;
  • Model output systems.

This model is a reference model in the construction of complex systems and focuses on the interaction with the external environment. This interaction is carried out by exposure to the system on the environment through the results of its targeted operation, ie through the desired product. Target system products - it exits the system. In turn, the external environment has an impact on the system through the provision of resource management and various controllable and uncontrollable factors that facilitate or impede the normal functioning of the system. Such links with the system of the environment (environmental impact on the system) called the input system. The scheme itself is depicted as a "non-transparent box", with the contents of the box were not disclosed. Therefore, the model is called the "black box."

To develop a model of the "black box" is necessary to determine the boundaries of the "process of assessing the quality of the development sites", the external environment, the ins and outs of the system.

The border of the "process of assessing the quality of the development sites" determined "website address";

As the external environment of the "quality evaluation process development sites" are:
Internet competitors, CMS, device users.

To exit the system, "the process of assessing the quality of the development sites" include:
